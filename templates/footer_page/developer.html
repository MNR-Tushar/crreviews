Development Journey

CR Reviews started as a personal project to solve a real problem I faced as a student. I noticed the lack of transparency in how CRs were performing and how students had no platform to share their experiences. After months of planning, coding, and testing, CR Reviews came to life.

The platform is built with modern web technologies focusing on security, anonymity, and user experience. Every feature is designed with students' needs in mind, from anonymous reviews to advanced search capabilities.
