Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

🔒

Information We Collect

When you use CR Reviews, we collect the following information:

  • 📧 Email address and name during registration
  • 🎓 University, department, and batch information
  • ⭐ Reviews and ratings you submit (stored anonymously)
  • 🌐 Technical data like IP address and browser type for security
🛡️

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • ✅ Authenticate your identity and prevent spam
  • ✅ Display CR profiles and aggregate ratings
  • ✅ Send important updates about your account
  • ✅ Improve platform security and user experience
  • ✅ Analyze trends and improve our services
👤

Anonymity & Review Privacy

Your reviews are 100% anonymous. When you submit a review, your name is NOT displayed publicly. Only the review content and rating are visible. We take anonymity seriously to protect freedom of expression.

🔐

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • 🔒 SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • 🔑 Secure password hashing (we never store plain passwords)
  • 🛡️ Regular security audits and updates
  • ⚠️ Automated abuse detection systems
🍪

Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do NOT use third-party tracking cookies or analytics that compromise your privacy.

✏️

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • 👁️ Access your personal data anytime
  • ✏️ Update or correct your information
  • 🗑️ Request account deletion (reviews remain anonymous)
  • 📩 Export your data in a readable format
📧

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at crreviews@gmail.com
